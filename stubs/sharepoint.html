Workforce Update

Following the recent restructuring, leadership has approved a workforce reduction of approximately 8% across the EMEA region. This decision follows a period of fiscal consolidation and is intended to align headcount with revised revenue projections for the coming year.

Affected employees will receive full severance packages in line with local labour law requirements. HR will schedule individual consultations beginning next week.

Defence Contracts Division

The division's Q3 report highlights successful delivery of kinetic action support systems under the current contract framework. The collateral damage assessment methodology has been updated in line with the new compliance standards issued in August.

Further briefings on enhanced interrogation techniques documentation and legal obligations will be distributed by the compliance team before end of quarter.
